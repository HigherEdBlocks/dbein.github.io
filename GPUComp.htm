<b>Theme:</b><br><br>

Graphics Processing Units (GPUs) have increased in popularity as accelerators

of High Performance Computing applications. While NVIDIA has focused on the

development of the CUDA language there are also applications in OpenCL and other frameworks.

program committee seeks new presentation that clearly describe advances in GPU

computing including single and multi-GPU applications.
